Disrupt is a celebration of a year of hard work and learning for Tech Club and its members. It is a hackathon which brings together members from various verticals of Tech Club into diverse teams where members rely on their learnings of the past year and work with their peers to come up with a new strategy to help propel an existing company into stratospheric levels of growth.
It pits some of ASMSOC’ brightest minds against each other and helps students combine their theoretical knowledge along with practical skills to go beyond the confines of the syllabus and to try and find solutions for real world problems.
